==============================================================
ON-DEVICE AI DIFFERENTIATION
==============================================================

KEY DIFFERENTIATOR (use early and often in pitches):
Three reasons on-device matters for pitch preparation:
1. Privacy: Pitches contain unreleased product info, sales strategy,
   and customer data that cannot go to third-party cloud APIs.
2. Latency: Real-time coaching requires < 200ms feedback loops.
   Cloud round-trips add 300–500ms for typical LLM APIs.
3. Trust: Users grant camera, mic, and screen access more willingly
   when they can verify data stays local.
